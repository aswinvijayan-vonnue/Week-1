Dal Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Yellow Dal
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 3 Green chillies
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2tbsp Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
    • Ghee
    • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
    • Fresh coriander leaves

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook the dal with 1.5 cups of water, a pinch of turmeric powder, and a few drops of oil for 15 minutes
  2. Saute the Aromatics: Heat oil in a pan for 2 min. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add the slit green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped onions.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  4. Pour the cooked dal into the pan. Mix well and let it simmer on low heat for 5 minutes so the flavors blend.
  5. Finish by stirring in freshly chopped coriander leaves

Glossary

Mushy
Soft and pulpy
Saute
Cook fd quickly in a small amount of fat or oil
